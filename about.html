Our Story

Built on Integrity, Powered by Experience

In 1986, Russ Loria started Loria Electrical Services with a single van and a simple promise — honest, fair, and fast electrical service for Rochester homeowners. A proud veteran, Russ brought the same discipline and work ethic from his military service into building a business the community could count on.

Nearly four decades later, that one van has grown into a fleet of service trucks staffed by a team of licensed, certified electricians. What hasn't changed is the commitment to doing the job right the first time, treating every customer's home like our own, and standing behind every piece of work.

Today, Loria Electrical Services is one of Rochester's most reputable electrical contractors, serving thousands of residential and commercial customers across Monroe County and beyond.
