Our Story

From One Van to Rochester's Most Trusted Electricians

In 1986, Russell "Russ" Loria started Loria Electrical Services with a single van and a commitment to doing things right. A veteran who brought the same discipline and integrity from his service to his work, Russ built the company on a simple promise: honest, fair, and fast service every single time.

Nearly four decades later, that one-van operation has grown into a fleet of trucks and a team of licensed master electricians serving thousands of homes and businesses across the Greater Rochester area. Through it all, the values that Russ built the company on have never changed — and neither has the personal attention every customer receives.
