Never be caught without power during Rochester's unpredictable winters. We install both portable and whole-home standby generator systems, properly sized for your property's needs and wired to code.

Emergency Electrical Service

Electrical emergencies don't wait for business hours. Our emergency line is answered around the clock, and we dispatch quickly to protect your home and family from electrical hazards.

Common emergencies we respond to:

  • Sudden or partial power outages
  • Burning smell from outlets or panel
  • Sparking or smoking outlets
  • Tripped breakers that won't reset
  • Storm or flood electrical damage
